Proof of Delivery

Health-Domain Execution

Three anonymized, board-level proof points from health and governance engagements. Client identifiers removed. Outcome types only — no fabricated metrics.

01

Health Reporting Overhaul

ISC/FNIHB Reporting · Governance · Compliance

A First Nations health department faced a persistent backlog in ISC/FNIHB reporting. Staff time was consumed by compliance tasks, creating delayed decisions and recurring audit pressure. Leadership lacked visibility into program performance.

  • Reporting audit and gap analysis
  • Standardized report templates (ISC/FNIHB-aligned)
  • Monthly reporting cadence and accountability structure
  • Capacity-building training for health staff

Reduced reporting backlog. Restored compliance confidence ahead of ISC review. Freed Health Director time for leadership priorities.

02

Mental Wellness Program Rebuild

Program Design · Mental Health · Addictions

A community-based mental wellness and addictions program lacked a defined service model, intake process, and outcome measurement framework. Staff roles were unclear and funder reporting was inconsistent.

  • Program logic model and theory of change
  • Service map and intake flow design
  • Staff role clarity and SOPs
  • KPI framework and outcome reporting template

Improved program consistency and client flow. Enabled funder reporting discipline. Reduced staff confusion and role overlap.

03

Health Budget Forecasting Discipline

Budget Management · Program Planning · Governance

A Health Director faced recurring mid-year budget pressure with no reliable forecasting model. Program managers lacked variance tracking tools, creating reactive rather than planned financial management.

  • Multi-year budget model with variance tracking
  • Program-level expenditure reporting tool
  • Monthly budget review cadence and reporting package
  • Training for program managers on budget discipline

Improved budget forecasting discipline. Reduced mid-year surprises. Strengthened Health Director confidence in financial reporting to Council and ISC.
